## Chauffeur-driven minibuses

Super Luxury VIP class Mercedes-Benz minibuses with an experienced chauffeur. Plush leather seating, ambient lighting, effective climate control and onboard entertainment systems are all designed to make any excursion, journey or trip comfortable and pleasant. The panoramic windows offer clear views for your travelling party. The privacy glass helps them relax from the stare of prying eyes too.

We offer 8, 11, 16 and 19 seater minibuses and 50 seater coaches. All with comfortable luxury seats, panoramic windows and the usual Mercedes-Benz refinements. Our VIP minibuses are ideal for transporting executives to events, functions and roadshows. Contact us for a no-obligation quotation for your group travel.
