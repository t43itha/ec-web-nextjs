## Impeccable safety and comfort

#### Conference style seating

The V-Class combines the sophistication and elegance expected from Mercedes-Benz vehicles with the practicality of a spacious interior, making it a popular choice for executive travel, airport transfers, corporate events, and sightseeing tours. The V-Class will give your party the space and flexibility to work, entertain or relax with its “conference” style seating arrangement. Our Extra Long models can carry 8 large suitcases.
