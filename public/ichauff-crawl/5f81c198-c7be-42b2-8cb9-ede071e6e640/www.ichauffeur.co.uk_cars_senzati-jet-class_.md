# Senzati Jet Class

#### The ultimate chauffeur-driven experience

## Jet Class

#### The chauffeur-driven commuting and leisure machine

With electric reclining heated massage seats in the rear you can sit back in sublime luxury. Featuring a middle console with two foldable tables and a fridge you can work, rest and play in style. Two screens and an Apple TV mean you can stay entertained on even the longest of journeys. For your privacy the Jet Class comes with side blackout curtains. Finished in sumptuous quilted Nappa leather and with a fiberoptic starlight roof you will experience supreme chauffeur-driven comfort.
